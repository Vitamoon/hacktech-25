About Us

01

What data sources do you use?

We pull water-level data from the USGS and housing trends from Zillow's public real estate databases, ensuring transparency and compliance.

02

Who benefits from this tool?

Homeowners, real estate investors, urban planners, and policymakers seeking to balance environmental risks with economic opportunities.

03

How accurate are your correlations?

We prioritize statistical rigor—models account for regional variables (e.g., local economy, population growth) to isolate water’s impact.

04

Can I use this for short-term decisions?

Yes! Our real-time water updates and quarterly housing data refresh help users act on timely insights.
